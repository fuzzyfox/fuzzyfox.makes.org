This is a poster on why red pandas are so awesome and cute.

At the Zoo

There are many red pandas that live in zoos.

Normally, when in zoos, red pandas are kept in large green spaces with lots of bushes and trees. Some red pandas really like to climb up into the trees to nap. They don't eat the tree leaves, but get fed bamboo leaves and fruits by the zookeepers.

Toronto Zoo

At the Toronzo Zoo, they live across the path from the new giant panda enclosure. Mozilla even had an outing to see them before the area was closed to create the panda area.

Did you know?

Red pandas sleep a lot. They move almost as little as sloths! During the day, they sleep on tree branches or in tree hollows.1 A new thing I learned!

Red pandas do love to play when they are active as you can see in the video. They are especially playful when they are young.

Even though in the video you saw them playing in the snow, they usually live in places with mild temperatures in forested mountain areas.
